Playsculpt Development Protocol
Protocol 1: Intellectual Property and Brands

To ensure a fair and legal creative environment for all users, Playsculpt enforces strict rules regarding the use of third-party intellectual property. All games, assets, and content created on the Playsculpt platform must be original or use content that you have the rights to.

Specifically, your game and its content must not be based on or include unauthorized use of real-world brands, characters, trademarks, or copyrighted material. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Company names and logos (e.g., Coca-Cola, Nike, McDonalds).
  • Characters from movies, TV shows, or other video games (e.g., Super Mario, Spongebob Squarepants).
  • Content that directly copies the "look and feel" of another property.

The goal of Playsculpt is to power imagination and originality. We encourage you to create your own unique brands and worlds!


Consequences for Violating DevProtocol 1

If our moderation team discovers that your game violates this protocol, the following actions will be taken:

1. Warning and Moderation Notice:
You will receive an official warning on your account with a notice explaining that your game has been moderated for violating DevProtocol 1.

2. Game is Force-Privated:
Your game will immediately be made private. While it will not be deleted from your account, no other users will be able to find or play it.

3. Required Action:
To have your game be made public again, you have the following options:

  • Option A: Revise the Game. You must remove all infringing content and change the game's theming to be original. Once you have made the necessary changes, you can submit the game for review by our moderation team.
  • Option B: Keep the Game Private. You may choose to not revise the game. In this case, it must remain private and cannot be made public.

Repeated violations of this protocol may lead to further account restrictions, including a temporary or permanent ban from the Service.

Thank you for helping us keep Playsculpt a creative and original platform.
